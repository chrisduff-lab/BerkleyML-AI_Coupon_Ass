## Summary of Findings
An analysis of coupons delivered to drivers.  This analysis provides insight into what coupon on driver conditions lead to the high or low coupon acceptance rate. This information will allow improved coupon targeting. 

**Bar Recommendation**
Based on the current data, 
- don't send coupons to drivers with kids, and
- do send more bar coupons to drivers who vist bars more than once a month.

**Coffee House Recommendations**
- Target coffee coupons to drivers who regularly visit coffee houses more than 1 time per month, with coupons that have an expiry of 1 day.
- Focus on short distance to coupon.  Generally >25min travel delivered low acceptance. Avoid these offers.
